Personal Envelopes

This is a website that allows you to keep track of your spending through what is known as the "envelopes" method. In this, you allocate a spending budget for each envelope each month, and you only spend from that envelope for its associated category. For example, all spending for groceries will come out of the "groceries" envelope. This method allows you to think about your spending and use your money more consciously. Enjoy!
